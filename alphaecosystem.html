Alpha Ecosystem®
technology framework for a decentralized internet


Alpha Ecosystem® is a highly scalable internet technology framework based on the idea of a decentralization internet that is indestructible, uninterruptible, and tamper-proof. Such an internet information system protects the privacy and ownership of digital assets (i.e., data) more effectively for every internet user. It empowers internet users to own, manage, and trade their digital assets, which are almost completely deprived away from the data owners and severely undervalued in the current centralized internet and economy.

The future of information technology is clearly showing two opposite trends. One is the unprecedented, highly centralized digital technology monopoly with big data and artificial intelligence as its core. The other is a decentralized technology system represented by blockchain technology. The current IT industry and the digital economy are already dominated by the few IT oligopolies and autocratic governments, in which very few business opportunities remain. On the other hand, the decentralized system is still in its infancy, which is a historical business opportunity to activate the enormous value of every internet user’s digital assets.

WeMake is one the very few technology companies that materializes the decentralization concept into concrete products and unleash the economic power of the digital assets for every digital citizen. Alpha Ecosystem® is creating/enabling a whole new economic sector for digital assets trading.

We are seizing this historic opportunity and focuses on the development and delivery of a robust minimal viable product (MVP), and developed the following four core technologies:


Core Technologies

  • Hypertext Bookmark: (more advanced patent compared to Samsung’s similar patent)
  • Smart Distributed Account Management System: (unique encrypted account on any user’s device, no registration or private information needed)
  • Peer-to-peer Decentralized Encrypted Communication
  • URL-free Encrypted Internet File Transfer Mechanism: (transfer files without URL)


Unique Advantages

  • Superior robustness, reliability, and scalability over the centralized systems and existing cloud systems.
  • Diminishing per megabit IT infrastructure operating cost.
  • Overall bandwidth efficiency and speed increase with the total number of users and user density.
  • Downward compatibility towards existing internet infrastructures, extremely low transfer cost.
  • Blockchain encrypted security, immutability, and privacy protection.
  • Total solution available for comprehensive projects from small to large scale institutional and governmental clients.
  • Particularly suitable for mobile and IoT, smart city integration.


High Tolerance to Less-than-Ideal Connectivity

Alpha Ecosystem®’s unique decentralized data processing, and storage mechanism enables the system to be very resilient to unstable or low-speed internet connectivity. Unlike other systems that require to stay constantly online to be functional, the applications developed by Alpha Ecosystem® only need to get access to the same piece of data once, then they can function independently of the internet connection. This unique characteristic is particularly suitable for both remote rural areas with poor internet infrastructure and the overcrowded urban data hot zone with digital traffic jams.
