Aki Talk
Mobile Learning Management System(LMS)

In the wake of global scale pandemic like COVID-19, digital learning is no longer a nice-to-have optional supplement but becomes an inevitable key component of formal schooling.

Aki Talk
Mobile Learning Platform

Aki Talk is an advanced digital Learning Management System (LMS) and Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) system. It is the world’s first LMS and MOOC system that is built with a decentralized curriculum, non-linear pedagogy, contextual, and assimilation learning mode. Compared to the transitional on-campus learning mode and other LMS with a centralized curriculum and linear pedagogical design, Aki Talk is superior in almost all aspects of teaching, learning, and operations, including efficiency, effectiveness, student learning outcome (SLO), flexibility, mobility, system robustness, and connectivity resilience.


Unique Features

Aki Talk is derived from battle-tested proven language learning systems and internationally recognized psychological findings, tailored for the fragmentized and individualized digital society. Aki Talk will become the most efficient language learning tool for today’s environment.

With Aki Talk, in merely 15~30 seconds, a learner can master a useful expression for real. All learning short videos come from the everyday pragmatics for real life scenarios. Through Aki Talk’s 15-30 second video clips, a language learner can master everyday expressions and phrases useful in real life scenarios.


Benefits to the Learners

  • Easy and simplistic: students learn by watching videos with real-life scenarios and full visual-audio brain stimulation. Compared to the memorization mode of learning, Aki Talk’s assimilation learning mode is much more intuitive and effective.
  • Mobile and flexible: students can learn anywhere, anytime with very flexible time and schedule, especially with fragmental time. Students don’t have to go through a fixed curriculum linearly, lesson by lesson, and level by level. Instead, students can learn individual knowledge points according to their own needs, pace, and time availability. It is particularly suitable for remote, blended, and self-paced learning scenarios.

  • Cost-efficient: save students’ monetary costs for data bandwidth, travel, lodging, and transportation. At the same time, it significantly improves student learning outcomes (SLO) and reduces study time.
  • Convenient: Tailored for mobile learning in the form of a mobile app. Much more convenient and practical for students than using a computer, especially in places where only mobile internet connectivity is available.
  • Interactive and integratedAki Talk is a full-function all-in-one LMS, with all necessary learning modules, including online learning, feedback, chatting, study log/checklist and etc. Students can accomplish online learning within Aki Talk, don’t need to juggle among different websites or apps for different functions.


Benefits to the Educational Institutions

  • Allows instructions off-campus without physical contact to comply with the social distancing policy.
  • Deploy, distribute, and content/instructions online via mobile and computer devices.
  • Reduce the overall instructional cost of the educational institutions
  • Significantly improved Program Learning Outcomes (PLO), Course Learning Outcomes (CLO) and Student Learning Outcomes(SLO)
  • Better protection of the universities’ intellectual property via ATK’s unique encryption and data storage technologies.

  • Extend the education coverage to the remote and inaccessible areas with a less-than-ideal internet connection.
  • First mobile-based LMS, more flexible and attractive to the younger generation students.
  • Reduce or decouple the institutions’ dependency on the general social media platform (e.g. Facebook) for recruiting, social marketing, and customer support.
